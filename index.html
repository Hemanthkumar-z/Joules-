I am wishing you plot twists that leave you breathless with delight, new passions that ignite your soul, and victories that echo across the mountains. So, to my favorite adventurer: Happy, Happy Birthday. May your days be kissed with endless wonder, your nights filled with deep, restorative peace, and your spirit forever alight with the wild, beautiful magic that makes you exactly who you are.
